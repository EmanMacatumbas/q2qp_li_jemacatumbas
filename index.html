Making the Marginalized Matter


Empowering, Uplifting, Inspiring, and Promoting the neglected Filipinos such as the PWD's

Background


This project stems from a young woman who opened her eyes to the harsh reality that many Filipinos face. Thus she, and many other humanitarians, formed a group and together aim to help and support underpriveleged and unrecognized groups such as people with disabilities.

"My advocacy is important to me because I believe true progress can be only achieved when it takes into account the well-being of all Filipinos including marginalized sectors such as the PWD community."

- Sam Tamayo

What we have Done so Far


At the beginning of this project, we have done small acts of Kindness such as leaving and donating food to the homeless, security guards, and food pantries. Now, ever since we have expanded, we have collaborated with other non-profit organizations to bring kindness to Filipinos across the country. We have also conducted online classes and meetings for the underpriveleged. We have also hosted multiple projects and activities for the talented Filipinos artists to help support groups like the farmers and fishermen. Due to the efforts of Project Smile, Thousands of Filipino lives today have improved .. one small act of kindness at a time.
