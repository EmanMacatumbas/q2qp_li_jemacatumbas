Initiatives


We are a non-profit ogranization who have contributed to the betterment of Filipino lives across the country.

Involvements


We have been involved in many different projects and activities such as ALKASAMOPI which is a PWD drivers association that advocates for safe and accessible roads. We also participate with the Philippine Children's Medical Center which is one of the government-owned and controlled corporate National Centers for Specialized Health Care under the Department of Health. There is also the building of the Welcome House which is recognized as the first crisis intervention center in the country. It is staffed by sisters and lay mission partners who are also professional guidance counselors and social workers where they conduct an outreach activity entitled GRLPWR together with our volunteers and partners from Pana.
